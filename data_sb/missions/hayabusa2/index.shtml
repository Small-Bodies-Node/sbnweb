Hayabusa2 Mission

Launched on 3 December 2014, the Hayabusa2 mission targeted the C-type asteroid (162173) Ryugu to investigate the origin of water and organic materials on Earth, and bring samples back to Earth. It performed an Earth flyby on 3 December 2015, arrived at Ryugu on 27 June 2018, and departed on 12 November 2019 before returning to Earth on 6 December 2020.

The SBN is the lead node for archiving data from Hayabusa2 into the PDS.
